Bio

Hi, I'm Mike. I am a web developer currently living in Seattle, WA. I specialize in design implementation and theme development. I have built sites in ModX and WordPress using responsive and adaptive design principles. In a past life I taught English as Second Language in Japan, Arizona and the Seattle area. Four years ago I decided to make a career switch and attended Seattle Central Community College to study Web Development. I began working full time as a professional web developer in January 2013 and have continued to take classes to keep my skills up and explore new technologies.


I have professional experience using HTML5, CSS3, PHP, ModX, Microsoft Azure, MySQL, Ubuntu and Linux, Apache, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, some Photoshop and Illustrator, and the Google Maps API. In addition to web development I also have experience in IT project management and Windows System Administration.


I'm currently at work building sites using node.js with Less, Express, Hapi, Backbone and an array of other javascript goodies.
